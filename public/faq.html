Bramp

Bramp is a payments app that allows users to make payments using both crypto and fiat. It is built to empower users with the right technologies for the future, while keeping everyday payments simple and accessible.

Bramp focuses on turning crypto into something practical and usable in daily life, not just an asset to hold.

What You Can Do With Bramp

  • Make fast and reliable payments using crypto or fiat from one app.
  • Pay for everyday utilities such as airtime, data, cable TV, and electricity using crypto or fiat.
  • Fund your wallet easily using crypto transfers or fiat options.
  • Enjoy lightning-fast transactions built on modern and reliable infrastructure.
  • Use AI-powered payments with natural language support to send money and complete transactions more easily.
  • Get access to 24/7 customer support whenever you need help.

Built for the Future of Payments

Bramp is designed to support real-world crypto usage, especially for retail payments. Our goal is to make it easy for users to spend crypto at stores, for services, and for everyday needs.

We believe crypto should be usable, simple, and integrated into daily life.

Our Vision

Bramp aims to help position Nigeria as a hub for retail crypto utility. We are building infrastructure that enables users and businesses to use crypto seamlessly for everyday transactions.

Our focus is on accessibility, speed, and real-world adoption.
