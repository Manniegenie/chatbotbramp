About Bramp

Bramp is a payments app that allows users to make payments using both crypto and fiat. It is built to empower users with the right technologies for the future, while keeping everyday payments simple and accessible. Bramp focuses on turning crypto into something practical and usable in daily life — not just an asset to hold.
  • Make fast and reliable payments using crypto or fiat from one app.
  • Pay for everyday utilities such as airtime, data, cable TV, and electricity using crypto or fiat.
  • Fund your wallet easily using crypto transfers or fiat options.
  • Enjoy lightning-fast transactions built on modern and reliable infrastructure.
  • Use AI-powered payments with natural language support to send money and complete transactions more easily.
  • Get access to 24/7 customer support whenever you need help.
Bramp is available on Android via Google Play. iOS is coming soon — join the waitlist using the App Store link on our homepage to be notified when it launches.

Payments & Vision
